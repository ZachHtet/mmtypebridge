Frequently Asked Questions

What is MM Type Bridge?
MM Type Bridge is a web tool that lets you easily convert between three major Myanmar text encoding formats: Unicode, Zawgyi, and Win Innwa. Just paste text in any format, and get instant conversions to all other formats.
What's the difference between Unicode, Zawgyi, and Win?
Unicode is the international standard for encoding text that supports all languages, including Myanmar. Zawgyi is a popular non-standard encoding in Myanmar that was used before Unicode support was widespread. Win Innwa is an older ASCII-based encoding system still used in some legacy systems and publications.
Why do I need to convert between these formats?
Different websites, applications, and legacy systems may use different encoding systems. When copying text between them, the characters may appear as boxes or display incorrectly. MM Type Bridge helps ensure your text displays correctly no matter where you use it.
Does MM Type Bridge store my text?
No, MM Type Bridge works entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server or stored anywhere. When you close the page, all text is cleared.
Which font format should I use?
Whenever possible, we recommend using Unicode as it's the international standard and has better support across modern devices and platforms. However, if you're working with older systems or specific websites that require Zawgyi or Win Innwa, use whichever format works for your situation.
